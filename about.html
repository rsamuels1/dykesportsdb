What is this?
THE IDEA

Dyke Sports DB is a community-built directory of sports clubs in Portland, OR for dykes, trans folks, and the broader queer community. Soccer leagues, flag football, basketball, ice hockey, and more — all in one searchable place.

Finding queer-friendly sports communities can be a word-of-mouth game. This makes it easier.

How it works
THE MODEL

Anyone can submit a club. Submissions are reviewed before going live to keep the directory accurate and relevant. Once approved, clubs are searchable and filterable by sport, skill level, and season.

No ads. No algorithms. Just a list.
