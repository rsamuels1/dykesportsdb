What is this?
THE IDEA

Queer Sports DB is a community-built directory of sports clubs for queers, trans folks, and the broader queer community. Soccer leagues, flag football, basketball, ice hockey, and more — all in one searchable place.

Finding queer-friendly sports communities hard. This makes it easier.

How it works
THE MODEL

Anyone can submit a club. Submissions are reviewed before going live to keep the directory accurate and relevant. Once approved, clubs are searchable and filterable by sport, skill level, and season.

No ads. No algorithms. Just a list.

Inclusivity
THE 🏳️‍⚧️ FLAG

Some clubs in this directory carry a trans flag — 🏳️‍⚧️ — next to their name. This isn't automatic. It means the club has made an explicit, public statement welcoming trans athletes: on their website, their Instagram, their bylaws, or somewhere people can actually find it.

A lot of these clubs are doing this in direct defiance of their national governing bodies. USA Powerlifting, USA Hockey, USA Rugby — many of the organizations these clubs technically fall under have policies that restrict or outright ban trans participation. These clubs have looked at those policies and said no. They've chosen their community over their federation, and they've put it in writing.

That's worth knowing. And it's worth showing up for those clubs.

Inclusivity
THE 🩷 FLAG

Some clubs carry a pink heart — 🩷 — next to their name. This means the club has specific language about being centered on lesbians and queer women. That might be in their name, their mission statement, their bylaws, or how they describe themselves online.

Lesbian-centered doesn't mean exclusive. These clubs welcome anyone who wants to play. It means they were built with lesbians and queer women at the center — and that framing matters for a lot of people looking for a home.
